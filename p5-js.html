Although these images look relatively simple, it required dozens of shapes and coordinates to carefully place each one for the final product to look the way it does. When reflecting upon each project I have done, I am sure that this one took the longest among them all.

It was less difficult to construct the Landscape due to the majority of the lines being geometric. The Self-Portrait, on the other hand, relied almost entirely on every type of circle imaginable: curves, arcs, semi-circles, and more.
