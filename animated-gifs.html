GIFS

To explore the art of GIF-making, I created two types of GIFs: Stop-Motion and Timeline. The difference between these lies in their animation styles: stop-motion GIFs are made up of an array of cycling photos. On the other hand, timeline GIFs' animations are smooth, for each component follows a pre-set path.

I think I enjoyed making Timeline GIFs more, since they are so versatile. Already, many of my friends have asked me if I can animate them gulping down the state of Florida just like the cat below (whose name is Zeus).
