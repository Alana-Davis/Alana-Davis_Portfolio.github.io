How many days has it been since August 28, 2025? That is the same number of days I have spent learning how to create and present digital art.

For the 7,481 days I have been alive before that date - at least those of which I was conscious for - I have been drawn towards traditional art. I thought the allure of digital art was buried solely in Photoshop, where camera filters and crisp colors could be applied to traditionally-crafted images.

However, what I now realize is that digital creation is its own form of art. From the patterns that reappear in code to the myriad of shortcuts and tools offered by illustration software, digital media does not echo what itâ€™s like to hold a pencil. It offers an entirely new route of human expression that cannot be compared, yet is equally as all-reaching. Now, I would like you to enjoy how I have chosen to participate in this type of creation.
